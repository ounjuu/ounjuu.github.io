Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

LunaFortune collects the following information to provide its services:

- Birth information: date of birth, time of birth, gender (entered by the user)
- Birthplace: place of birth (optional, for astrology services)

All information is stored only on the user's device (local storage) and is never transmitted to or stored on external servers.

2. Purpose of Collection

- To provide tarot, saju (Korean fortune telling), and astrology services
- To generate AI-powered fortune readings using birth information
- To enable sharing of fortune reading results as images

3. Data Retention and Deletion

- All personal data is stored only on the user's device.
- Deleting the app automatically removes all stored data.
- Users can delete their data at any time through the app settings.
- No login or account is required. Simply deleting the app removes all data completely.

4. Third-Party Sharing

LunaFortune does not share personal information with third parties.

However, birth information is sent to AI services (Google Gemini, Groq) solely for generating fortune readings. These services do not store the information.

5. Advertising

LunaFortune includes Google AdMob advertisements.

- Advertising identifiers may be used for ad delivery.
- Advertising identifiers are managed by the device and can be reset or disabled in device settings.
- No personally identifiable information is provided to advertising services.

6. User Rights

- Users can view, modify, or delete their birth information in the app settings at any time.
- All data can be completely removed by deleting the app.
- No account registration or login is required, so no withdrawal process is needed.

7. Security Measures

- Communication with AI services is encrypted via HTTPS.
- Personal data is stored only on the device, minimizing the risk of external exposure.
- No account system means no risk of account compromise.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be communicated through app updates.
